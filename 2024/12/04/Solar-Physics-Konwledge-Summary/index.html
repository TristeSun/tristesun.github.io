太阳物理课程期末考试复习纲要

Global Parameters of the Sun

Based on Physics of the Sun: A First Course, chapter 1.

Orbit motion of the Earth

Kepler’s third law:$$\begin{equation}\frac{GM_{\odot}}{D^3}=\frac{4\pi^2}{T^2}\end{equation}$$

Astronomical Unit (AU)

$$\begin{equation}1 \mathrm{AU}=149.6 \times 10^6 \mathrm{km}\end{equation}$$
在1AU的距离下，1角秒可以表示为
$$\begin{equation}1\text{arc sec}=728.8\mathrm{km}\end{equation}$$在AU和年的单位下，Kepler’s third law可以表示为
$$\begin{equation}T^2(\mathrm{year})=D^3(\mathrm{AU})\end{equation}$$

$GM_{\odot}$ and the Mass of the Sun

Power Output of the Sun: The Solar Luminosity

The flux of radiant energy coming from the Sun: the solar irradiance, $I_s\approx 1366\mathrm{W}/\mathrm{m}^{-2}=1.366\times 10^6\mathrm{erg}\mathrm{s}^{-1}\mathrm{cm}^{-2}$
$$\begin{equation}L_{\odot}=4\pi D^2I_s=3.8416\times 10^{33}\mathrm{erg}/\mathrm{s}\end{equation}$$太阳的能量衰减
$$\begin{equation}\frac{dM}{dt}=4.274\times 10^{12}\mathrm{gm}/\mathrm{s}\end{equation}$$

Radius of the Sun: $R_{\odot}$

$$\begin{equation}R_{\odot}\approx 6.96\times 10^5\mathrm{km}=959\text{arc sec}\end{equation}$$
$$\begin{equation}1\mathrm{AU}=215.04R_{\odot}\end{equation}$$

Surface Gravity of the Sun

Escape Speed from the Solar Surface

$$\begin{equation}V_e=\sqrt{\frac{2GM_{\odot}}{R_{\odot}}}=617.7\mathrm{km}/\mathrm{s}\end{equation}$$

Effective Temperature of the Sun

$$\begin{equation}L_{\odot}=4\pi R_{\odot}^2\sigma_{B}T_{eff}^4\end{equation}$$

Shape of the Sun

纬度较差自转 latitudinal differential rotation:$$\begin{equation}\Omega(\lambda)=\Omega(0)[1-b\sin^2\theta-c\sin^4\theta]\end{equation}$$

Critical Frequency for Solar Oscillations

太阳震荡的周期 $$\begin{equation}T_g=2\pi \sqrt{\frac{R_{\odot}^3}{GM_{\odot}}}\end{equation}$$
因此震荡的频率可写为$$\begin{equation}\nu_g=\frac{1}{T_g}=99.92\mu\mathrm{Hz}\end{equation}$$

Mean Density of the Sun

$$\begin{equation}\bar{\rho}=\frac{M_{\odot}}{(4/3)\pi R_{\odot}^3}=1.410\mathrm{gm}/\mathrm{cm}^3\end{equation}$$
气态太阳的平均密度略大于液态水。

Exercise

  1. Consult a table of orbital periods for the planets Mercury, Mars, Jupiter, and Neptune. Using Kepler’s third law, determine the mean distance of each planet from the Sun in AU and in cm.
    查阅水星、火星、木星和海王星行星的轨道周期表。使用开普勒第三定律，确定每颗行星与太阳的平均距离（以 AU 和 cm 为单位）。
    解：
    $$\begin{equation}D=\sqrt[3]{\frac{GM_{\odot}T^2}{4\pi^2}}\end{equation}$$

  2. We shall see (Chapter 18) that the Sun’s influence over the surrounding space extends out as far as about 100 AU. Determine the period (in years) that a planet would have if it were in an orbit with that value of D. Assuming the orbit is circular, determine the speed of the planet in its orbit.
    我们将看到（第 18 章）太阳对周围空间的影响延伸到大约 100 AU。确定如果行星位于具有该值为 D 的轨道上，则行星将具有的周期（以年为单位）。假设轨道是圆形的，则确定行星在其轨道上的速度。
    解：
    $$\begin{equation}T^2(\text{year})=D^3(\text{AU})\end{equation}$$
    $$\begin{equation}\Rightarrow T=\sqrt{D^3}=1000\text{year}\end{equation}$$
    又由$\dfrac{v^2}{D}=\dfrac{4\pi^2 D}{T^2}$
    $$\begin{equation}\Rightarrow v=\frac{2\pi D}{T}\end{equation}$$

    注意单位换算

  3. The Sun is currently estimated to be some 4.6 Gy old. When the Sun was younger than 1 Gy, theory suggests that its luminosity was only 70% of what it is today. Assuming that the Sun’s radius has not changed, calculate the effective temperature of the young Sun.
    目前估计太阳大约有 4.6 Gy 的历史。当太阳小于 1 Gy 时，理论表明它的光度只有今天的 70%。假设太阳的半径没有变化，计算年轻太阳的有效温度。
    解： 由$L_{\odot}=4\pi R_{\odot}^2\sigma_{B}T_{eff}^4$
    $$\begin{equation}\Rightarrow T_{eff,young}=\sqrt[4]{0.7}T_{eff}\end{equation}$$

  4. Using information provided above, determine the mean density of the Earth. Given the scaling $T_g\sim \sqrt{\bar{\rho}}$, calculate $T_g$ for the Earth. How does $P_g$ compare with the orbital period of the RADCAT satellite in its near-Earth orbit?
    使用上面提供的信息，确定地球的平均密度。给定缩放比例 $T_g\sim \sqrt{\bar{\rho}}$，计算地球的 $T_g$。$T_g$ 与 RADCAT 卫星在其近地轨道上的轨道周期相比如何？
    解： 见上

  5. Stars belonging to a feature called “the main sequence” have radii $R_*$ and masses $M_∗$ which scale roughly as $R_∗ = R_{\odot} (M_∗/M_{\odot} )^{0.7}$. For stars with masses 0.1, 0.3, 1, 3, and 10 $M_{\odot}$ on the main sequence, calculate $R_∗$, and evaluate the surface gravity (Equation 1.13) and the escape speed $V_{esc}$ from the surface (Equation 1.14).
    属于一个称为“主序”的特征的恒星的半径为 $R_*$ 和质量为 $M_∗$大致为 $R_∗ = R_{\odot} (M_∗/M_{\odot} )^{0.7}$。对于主序上质量为 0.1、0.3、1、3 和 10 M 的恒星，计算 $R_*$，并评估表面重力（方程 1.13）和从表面逃逸速度 $V_{esc}$（方程 1.14）。
    解：
    $$\begin{equation}g=\frac{GM}{R^2}\end{equation}$$
    $$\begin{equation}V_{esc}=\sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}}\end{equation}$$

  6. The masses and luminosities of main sequence stars can be approximated by $L_∗ \sim M_∗^{3.8}$. Using the formula for luminosity in Section 1.8, and the $R_∗ − M_∗$ formula in Exercise 5, show that $T_{eff}$ for main sequence stars scales as $M_∗^{0.6}$. Using this scaling along with Equation 1.15, calculate $T_{eff}$ for main sequence stars with masses of 0.1, 0.3, 1, 3, and 10 $M_{\odot}$ .
    主序星的质量和光度可以用 $L_∗ \sim M_∗^{3.8}$ 来近似。使用第 1.8 节中的光度公式和练习 5 中的 $R_∗ − M_∗$ 公式，可以看出主序星的 $T_{eff}$ 比例为 $M_∗^{0.6}$。使用此缩放和方程 1.15，计算质量为 0.1、0.3、1、3 和 10 $M_{\odot}$ 的主序星的 $T_{eff}$ 。
    解：
    $$M_*^{0.7\times 2}T^4\sim M_*^{3.8}$$
    $$T\sim M_*^{0.6}$$
